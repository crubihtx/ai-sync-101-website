# AI Sync 101 Website - Complete Overview

## Project Summary

A modern, professional single-page website for AI Sync 101 that positions the company as a technology partner with 20+ years of operational expertise - not just another "AI company."

**Key Positioning:** From break/fix IT → MSP → Cybersecurity → Software Development → AI Integration

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimal design inspired by Stripe, Vercel, and Linear
- **Color Scheme:** Blue-to-pink gradient from logo (#00A3FF → #FF1F8F)
- **Typography:** Inter font family, high contrast, generous whitespace
- **Animations:** Subtle scroll-based fade-ins and micro-interactions
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first design, fully responsive across all devices

### Brand Identity
- **Not an AI company** - Technology company with operational expertise
- **Sister company to Louisiana Computer Technologies**
- **20+ years of experience** solving real operational problems
- **SOC 2 certified infrastructure**
- **Problem-solver first, technology second**

## Brand Guidelines

### Colors
- **Primary Blue:** #00A3FF (rgb(0, 163, 255))
- **Primary Pink:** #FF1F8F (rgb(255, 31, 143))
- **Gradient:** 135deg from Blue to Pink
- **Black:** #0A0A0A
- **White:** #FFFFFF
- **Grays:** Scale from #1A1A1A to #F5F5F5

### Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (fallback: system fonts)
- **Headings:** Weight 600-700, tight line-height (1.2)
- **Body:** Weight 400, comfortable line-height (1.6)
- **Sizes:** Responsive with clamp() for fluid typography

### Spacing
- **Consistent scale:** 0.5rem, 1rem, 2rem, 4rem, 6rem, 8rem
- **Section padding:** 8rem vertical (5rem on mobile)
- **Generous whitespace** between all elements

### UI Elements
- **Border radius:** 8px standard, 12px for cards
- **Shadows:** Subtle, layered shadows
- **Buttons:** Gradient background, rounded corners, hover lift
- **Cards:** White background, subtle border, hover effects
