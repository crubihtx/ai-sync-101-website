Common Questions

Clear answers about how we work

What makes you different from dev shops?

We're not a development shop — we're an operations company that builds software. We've spent two decades running IT operations, managing infrastructure, and solving real business problems. We understand operational efficiency, workflow design, and system integration from hands-on experience, not just technical implementation.

Why not just use AI tools ourselves?

Off-the-shelf AI tools solve generic problems. Your operational challenges are specific to your business processes, existing systems, and workflow requirements. We build custom platforms that integrate with your existing infrastructure and solve your exact problem — not a close approximation.

What if we already have [Tool X]?

Good. We integrate with it. Most of our projects involve connecting existing tools, filling gaps where commercial software doesn't fit, and building the "glue layer" between systems. We're not here to replace working tools — we solve problems your current stack can't handle.

How do you price projects?

Projects are priced based on measurable impact. If we can't identify significant annual impact (time savings, revenue recovery, cost reduction), we'll tell you upfront. Development happens in predictable phases with clear deliverables. No long-term contracts — just working software that solves real problems.

What happens after deployment?

Monthly ongoing services include infrastructure management, security updates, monitoring, and support. Your platform runs on enterprise-grade infrastructure with industry-standard security. We handle the technical operations so you can focus on using the system. Additional features are developed in predictable phases as needed.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. We work with mid-market companies nationwide. Custom software development and operational automation don't require physical presence — we work remotely with clients across the US who have complex operational challenges.
